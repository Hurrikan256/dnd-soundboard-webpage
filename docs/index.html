DnD Soundboard – Music & SFX

Bring Your Tabletop Adventures to Life!

Whether you’re a Dungeon Master or a Game Master, DnD Soundboard – Music & SFX gives you everything you need to create an immersive audio atmosphere for your players. Designed specifically for live tabletop sessions, the app makes it effortless to trigger music, ambience, and special effects on the fly.

Cinematic experience at your fingertips!

1. Powerful, Fast, and Table-Ready

This DnD Soundboard delivers instant, reliable playback with a clean, efficient interface designed for real game sessions. Sounds trigger without delay, categories keep everything organized, and offline functionality ensures you’re ready anywhere. Whether reacting to player choices or building atmosphere, the app gives you fast, dependable control at the table.

2. Preset Sounds and Custom Imports

Start immediately with high-quality preset ambience, combat, magic, and creature sounds. When you need more, import your own audio and organize it into custom categories. Everything integrates seamlessly for quick access and smooth playback, allowing you to tailor the soundboard perfectly to your campaign’s tone and style.

3. Full Control for Dynamic Storytelling

Adjust individual volumes, layer sounds, loop ambience, or trigger quick one-shots without interruptions. Pitch shifting adds dramatic variation, and instant scene switching keeps pacing tight. This level of control lets you enhance mood, tension, and immersion, turning your tabletop encounters into cinematic, memorable moments that feel truly alive.

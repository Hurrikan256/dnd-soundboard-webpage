Three Audio Layers

Bring your adventure to life with three independent audio layers: music, ambience, and sound effects. Play them simultaneously, switch tracks instantly, and keep everything organized by category. With just a tap, you can shift the mood, trigger dramatic moments, or build tension exactly when you need it.

Fully Customizable Buttons

Design your soundboard your way. Assign built-in sounds or upload your own audio and images. Edit button text, fine-tune volume, and even adjust pitch for effects to match the scene. Every button is fully customizable, giving you total control over how you trigger sounds at the table.

Advanced Control & Presets

Each audio layer has its own volume slider for the perfect mix. Add or remove buttons within the music, ambience, and effects tabs to shape your workspace. Create multiple presets, save complete soundboard setups, and switch between them instantly—ideal for campaigns, one-shots, or different storytelling moods.
